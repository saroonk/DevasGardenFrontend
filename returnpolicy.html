Damaged on Arrival?

Plants are delicate travelers. If your plant arrives with significant structural damage, broken stems, or severe wilting, we will make it right.

Replacement Only

To keep our garden growing, we offer replacements only for damaged items. We do not issue monetary refunds once a plant has been shipped.

48-Hour Guarantee

Please inspect your green friends immediately. Claims must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery to qualify for a replacement.

How to request a replacement:

  • 02. Go to Track Your Order Page, There you can see the deliverded product with a option to Return. Click and fill out the form.
  • 03. Our botanists will review the claim and ship your new plant within 5-7 business days.
